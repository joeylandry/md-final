About Me

I am passionate about speaking to youth and adults about God and leading them closer in their relationship with Christ. I have worked as a High School Theology Teacher, Counselor, and Adult Confirmation Coordinator. I love helping people go deeper in their faith in any way.

If you need a talk, retreat, or parish mission for any age, please reach out to me and I’d love to speak at your parish or school.

BeLoved Women's Retreat

A retreat for teens and adults to discover their true identity, worth, and beauty as the beloved daughter of God. It's a weekend like no other where women experience the unconditional love of God!

The retreat is available as a 1, 2, or 3 day retreat on the following topics: Identity as beloved daughter, God as Father, Beauty, Women in the Bible, Bride of Christ, and The Feminine Genius.

*These are also available as individual talks.
